Coding

Throughout college and his professional career, Drew has spent much of his personal time coding, and finding ways to integrate his coding interests into his professional life (including building a slack app that intelligently replies to barista requests with recipes for menu drinks at Gregorys). In 2020, Drew decided to actively pursue software development as a career. After some research, he applied and was accepted into App Academy, a highly selective and rigorous boot camp that spans 16 weeks. From July 2020 until late October, Drew spent 70-100 hours/week fine tuning his knowledge in Javascript, React, Redux, Ruby, and Rails, earning a 99.8% assessment average over that time.
