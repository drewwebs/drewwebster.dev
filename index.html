Drew Webster

Software Developer

Background

Prior to becoming a developer, Drew worked at Gregorys Coffee, a chain of 32 cafes totaling 300+ employees. Thanks to his drive, passion, reliability, efficiency, innovation, and teambuilding skills; he was promoted 6 times in 4 years, from an hourly barista to the Director of Employee Experience. This experience imbued in him strong leadership skills, as well as collaborative, problem solving, and organizational skills. It also taught him to keep calm and remain methodical under pressure, resulting in clean and concise code even when under strict deadlines.

Coding

Throughout college and his professional career, Drew has spent much of his personal time coding, and finding ways to integrate his coding interests into his professional life. At Gregorys Coffee, he designed and deployed a slack app that leverages Node.js to intelligently reply to FAQs from Baristas, putting valuable information at their fingertips and directly driving increased productivity. In 2020, Drew applied and to App Academy, a highly selective and rigorous boot camp that spans 16 weeks. From July 2020 until late October, Drew spent 70-100 hours/week fine tuning his knowledge in Javascript, React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, HTML5, and CSS3, earning a 99.8% assessment average over that time.
